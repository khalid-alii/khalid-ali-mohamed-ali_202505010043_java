**2.1. Why are the variables declared as private?**
To enforces **encapsulation**, it blocks access to the variables from outside the `Student` class. This protects the data from unwanted modification.

**2.2. What happens if the variables are declared public?**
Any other class can directly access and modify them.


**2.3. Why do we use getters and setters?**
Getters and setters provide a controlled public interface to access and update the private data.
